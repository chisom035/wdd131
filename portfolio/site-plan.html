Overview

Purpose

To showcase my software engineering skills, projects, and experience to potential employers, clients, and collaborators. The portfolio will demonstrate my technical abilities, design sense, and problem-solving capabilities through real-world projects and case studies.

Audience

Tech recruiters, hiring managers, potential clients, fellow developers, and anyone interested in my work. The primary audience includes professionals in the tech industry looking to assess my skills for employment or collaboration opportunities.

Dynamic elements

Interactive project filtering, animated statistics counters, form validation, smooth scrolling navigation, responsive mobile menu, dynamic content loading for projects and skills, and interactive UI elements that enhance user experience.

Content

Home page

The home page will feature a hero section with my introduction, skills overview, featured projects, about me section, and contact information. It will showcase my best work and provide easy navigation to different sections of the portfolio.

Projects

The projects page will display all my projects in a filterable grid layout. Each project will include a description, technologies used, and links to live demos and source code. Users can filter projects by technology or category.
